Site Name

GPCS

The name Guayaquil Pacific Chamber Society describes the numerous people that live in the city of Guayaquil, which is also known as the "Pearl of the Pacific". These two names give an identity to the page and also describe the city where it is found. I decided to use "Society" because the meaning is significant to the people in my city a club formed for a purpose. For these reasons, I chose this name.

Site Purpose

Purpose

The Guayaquil Pacific Chamber Society acts as a center that offers workshops, seminars, and training programs to help the members of the society improve their sales, client service, and the implementation of new technologies in their businesses, such as the use of AI. Also, we will work on the Networking Opportunities for small entrepreneurs to expand their contacts, learn from other businesses with experience, and find new opportunities of promoting products for sale or purchase.
